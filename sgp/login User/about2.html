Drop Shipping

Drop shipping is a streamlined form of retail business wherein the seller accepts customer orders but does not keep goods sold in stock.Instead, in a form of supply chain management, it transfers the orders and their shipment details to either the manufacturer, a wholesaler, another retailer, or a fulfillment house, which then ships the goods directly to the customer.
